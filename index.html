Welcome to Bavarian Motors Workshop

We take care of everything in your automobile for you

Bavarian auto Car Care is a full-service auto repair, auto maintenance and tire service shop in Colombo, Sri lanka. We are family owned and operated and have been dedicated to providing top quality auto repair services in Muncie, IN and the surrounding communities since 1990 – more than 20 years! All of our mechanics are ASE Certified Technicians and have more than 102 years of combined automotive industry experience. We utilize state-of-the-art technology and diagnostic equipment to provide a wide range of auto repair services on all makes and models of vehicles. From oil changes and brake repair to computerized tune-ups, transmission services and tire services, we’ve got you covered! We are also pleased to announce that we now sell tires in Muncie! We sell and service tires for all vehicle makes and models and guarantee low prices. In addition, our auto parts are backed by a 12 month/12,000 mile warranty, and we have multiple tire warranties, so you can be completely comfortable bringing your car to Bavarian auto Car Care.
